# BrikByte Studios — Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Pledge
We, as members, contributors, and leaders of the BrikByte Studios open ecosystem, pledge to make participation in our projects and community a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of:
- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
- Sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an **open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community**.

---

## 2. Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people.  
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.  
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.  
- Taking responsibility for our mistakes and apologizing when necessary.  
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.  
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.  
- Public or private harassment.  
- Publishing others’ private information (such as physical or email addresses) without explicit permission.  
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## 3. Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers, DevOps Leads, and core contributors are responsible for:
- Clarifying and enforcing community standards.
- Taking appropriate corrective action for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.  
- Communicating enforcement decisions clearly and consistently.

The BrikByte **Security & Compliance team** may be consulted for escalations involving:
- Repeated violations or code-of-conduct abuse.
- Harassment or security threats.
- Organization-wide behavioral concerns.

---

## 4. Scope
This Code of Conduct applies:
- Within all BrikByte project spaces (repositories, issue trackers, PRs, documentation portals, chat platforms).  
- In public spaces when an individual is representing BrikByte or its community.

Examples of representation:
- Using an official project e-mail address or social handle.  
- Acting as a project representative at online/offline events.

---

## 5. Enforcement Guidelines
Enforcement actions will be guided by the following community impact scale:

| Level | Community Impact | Consequence |
|-------|------------------|--------------|
| **1. Correction** | Use of inappropriate language or behavior deemed unprofessional. | A private written warning from maintainers, explaining why the behavior was inappropriate and outlining expectations for improvement. |
| **2. Warning** | A single incident or series of non-severe violations. | A formal warning and requirement to cease behavior. Temporary exclusion from community channels may apply. |
| **3. Temporary Ban** | Serious violations, sustained disruptive behavior, or harassment. | Temporary exclusion from all BrikByte community spaces for a defined period. Conditions for return will be communicated. |
| **4. Permanent Ban** | Demonstrated pattern of violation, harassment, or malicious intent. | Permanent removal from all BrikByte community participation spaces. |

---

## 6. Reporting Guidelines
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
1. **Report confidentially** to: [security@brikbyte.dev](mailto:security@brikbyte.dev)  
2. Provide:
   - The nature of the incident,
   - Where it occurred (repo / issue / PR / chat),
   - Any supporting screenshots or evidence.
3. The Security team will acknowledge receipt within **2 business days**, review within **10 days**, and take appropriate action.  
4. Reporters will receive updates until resolution.

All reports will be handled with **respect for the reporter’s privacy** and in accordance with applicable data-protection regulations.
